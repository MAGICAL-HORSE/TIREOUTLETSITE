Tire Tips & Advice

From the Shop Floor

Buying Guide

New vs. Used Tires in Houston — Which Is Right for You?

Used tires are a smart choice when your budget is tight and your car isn't new. We inspect every used tire for tread depth, sidewall condition, and even load rating before it goes on your vehicle. For most Houston driving — highways and surface streets — a good used tire from $45 will serve you just as well as a new one at $90+. That said, if you drive a lot of highway miles or own a newer car, investing in new tires makes sense. Our team will tell you honestly which one fits your situation.

— Tire Outlet Team
Maintenance

How Houston Heat Destroys Tires Faster Than Anywhere Else

Houston asphalt can hit 170°F in summer. That heat accelerates rubber degradation, causes blowouts, and ages tires 30–40% faster than cooler climates. Signs your tires are heat-damaged: cracking on the sidewall, bulges, or tread that looks worn unevenly. We recommend Houston drivers check tire pressure monthly — heat causes it to spike, and overinflation is one of the top causes of blowouts. Stop by and we'll check it for free.

— Tire Outlet Team
Suspension

5 Signs Your Suspension Needs Work — Houston Roads Are Rough

Houston's roads and railroad crossings are brutal on suspension. Watch for: (1) Bouncing excessively after bumps — worn shocks. (2) Pulling to one side — alignment or control arm issue. (3) Nose diving when braking — struts. (4) Uneven tire wear — alignment or worn bushings. (5) Clunking noises over bumps — ball joints or control arms. Any of these? Come see us — we do full suspension diagnostics and repair right here on Westheimer.

— Tire Outlet Team
