FINANCING FAQ

Common questions about financing at Tire Outlet

Do I need good credit to apply?

No. Both Snap Finance and Acima are lease-to-own programs that do not require traditional credit. They look at factors like your income and bank account history rather than your credit score.

Will applying affect my credit score?

Snap Finance and Acima typically do not perform a hard credit pull during the initial application, so applying should not impact your credit score. Check each partner's terms for full details.

How quickly can I get approved?

Many customers are approved within minutes of submitting their application online. You can apply before coming in, or we can help you apply right here at the shop.

What's the minimum purchase amount?

Minimum amounts may vary by program. Call us at (281) 902-7290 or stop by and we can walk you through which option works best for your purchase.

Can I pay it off early?

Yes — both Snap and Acima offer early payoff options that can reduce the total cost. Paying off sooner is usually the better deal. Ask us about 90-day same-as-cash options.
